## Advanced Optimization for 2026 Hardware

To achieve the best performance in Road to Vostok, players should look beyond the in-game settings menu. Modifying launch arguments and system-level graphics preferences can significantly stabilize frame rates in dense forest areas or urban ruins.

### Launch Argument Modifications
You can input specific commands into the Steam Launch Options to force the game engine into a more stable rendering mode.

1. Right-click **Road to Vostok** in your Steam Library.
2. Select **Properties** > **General**.
3. Locate the **Launch Options** field.
4. Enter one of the following commands:
   - `-dx11` (For older hardware stability)
   - `-dx12` (For modern GPU feature sets)
   - `-windowed -noborder` (To fix multi-monitor scaling issues)

### Dedicated Graphics Settings
Many laptop users accidentally run the game on integrated graphics. To fix this, search for "Graphics Settings" in Windows, add the Road to Vostok executable, and set it to **High Performance**. This ensures the game utilizes your dedicated GPU rather than the CPU's internal chip.
