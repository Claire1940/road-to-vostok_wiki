## How to Find the Legendary Tunnel Key

Unlike standard loot which can be found in abundance, the Tunnel Key is classified as a **Legendary** item. This means its spawn rate is significantly lower than common house keys or industrial keys. Based on current exploration data from 2026, the primary location for discovering this key is the **Village map**.

Survivors should focus their scavenging efforts on residential buildings and specialized loot containers within the Village. The key does not have a fixed "static" spawn point; instead, it appears randomly in lootable boxes, drawers, and cabinets. It is highly recommended to clear the Village thoroughly before transitioning to the Highway, as the Highway map presents a much higher threat level from hostile AI bandits.

> **Warning:** The Tunnel Key is a single-use item. Once you interact with the locked door in the Highway tunnel, the key is consumed from your inventory. Ensure you have ample backpack space before heading to the lockbox.

## Locating the Secret Tunnel on the Highway

Once you have secured the key, your next objective is the **Highway map**. This map is characterized by long sightlines, abandoned vehicles, and high-density AI patrols. The tunnel itself is an underground area located near the main highway infrastructure, often found beneath or adjacent to large bridge sections.

To reach the treasure room, follow these steps:
1.  **Navigate to the Highway Map:** Ensure you are well-equipped with at least a submachine gun or rifle, as the bandits here often carry AKs and rare backpacks.
2.  **Locate the Bridge Area:** Look for the small underground entrance located near the highway's structural supports.
3.  **Identify the Locked Door:** Inside the initial tunnel area, there is a specific door that prompts the "Open with Tunnel Key" interaction.
4.  **Clear the Perimeter:** AI bandits frequently patrol the entrance to the tunnel. It is safer to eliminate them from a distance before attempting to unlock the door.

## Loot Rewards and Treasure Room Contents

The legendary status of the tunnel key is well-earned. Inside the locked bunker, players will find a mix of military-grade hardware and essential survival supplies. The room functions as a hybrid between a treasure room and a temporary shelter, though it is currently most valuable for its "Special Crate."

### Weaponry and Tactical Gear
Inside the bunker, you are likely to find high-tier firearms that are difficult to locate in the early game. The **MP5K** is a common find on the floor or within crates in this area, providing a compact and reliable secondary weapon. Additionally, shotguns and various pistol magazines are frequently stocked here.

### The Special Crate
The centerpiece of the room is the **Special Crate**. Unlike standard military crates which may be empty, the Special Crate is guaranteed to contain a high density of loot.

## Survival Crafting: The Mess Kit

One of the most important items found within the tunnel is the **Mess Kit**. This is a critical tool for long-term survival in Road To Vostok. Once you have the Mess Kit, you can access advanced crafting recipes at your base or campfire.

The Mess Kit allows you to process raw ingredients into nutritious meals and stimulants. In the 2026 version of the game, maintaining your hunger and hydration levels is vital for stamina regeneration.

**Common Mess Kit Recipes:**
*   **Coffee:** Requires Water, Coffee Grounds, Filter, and the Mess Kit. Provides a significant stamina boost.
*   **Pea Soup:** Requires Water, Pea Soup (can), and the Mess Kit. High calorie and hydration recovery.
*   **Fish/Tomato Soup:** Requires specialized ingredients and the Mess Kit for preparation.

> **Tip:** The Mess Kit is a permanent tool. It does not disappear after one use, making it one of the best investments for your inventory space when returning from the Highway.

## Secondary Loot Locations: The Highway Supermarket

If your backpack isn't full after looting the tunnel, the Highway map offers another high-value location: the **Supermarket**. While it doesn't require a legendary key, it is heavily guarded by bandits.

The Supermarket is the best place to find "Large Med Kits," which are rare items capable of healing significant damage. You can also find "num nums" (snacks), juice, and various clothing items. Because the bandits in this area often wear **Patrol Backpacks** or **Dino Backpacks**, it is an excellent spot to upgrade your carrying capacity. The Patrol Backpack, for instance, offers a +20kg or +30kg carrying capacity depending on its rarity, which is essential for hauling the heavy batteries and cables found in the tunnel.

## Tactical Advice for the Highway Map

The Highway is a "no-man's land" where the AI is significantly more aggressive than in the Village. When searching for the tunnel entrance, keep these tips in mind:

1.  **Check Your Ammo:** Bandits on the Highway often carry AK-style rifles. If you are using a Mosin, ensure you have a scope and plenty of spare rounds, as long-range engagements are common.
2.  **Inventory Management:** Items like the Large Battery and Mess Kit take up significant grid space. Prioritize dropping low-value items like basic shoes or common clothes to make room for legendary loot.
3.  **Use Cover:** The Highway is open and exposed. Move between abandoned cars and use the bridge pillars for cover when approaching the tunnel entrance.
4.  **Listen for Footsteps:** The underground tunnel area has distinct acoustics. You can often hear AI bandits moving above you or near the entrance before you see them.
