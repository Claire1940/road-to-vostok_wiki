## Outpost Map Layout and Key Locations

The Outpost map is a transition zone that connects the School and the Highway to the more dangerous Minefield area. Unlike the claustrophobic corridors of the School, the Outpost is wide and open, demanding a different tactical approach. Players must contend with long sightlines and minimal cover in the central fields.

| Location | Description | Key Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Sniper Tower** | A tall wooden structure on the map's edge. | **High-tier loot crates**, 9x19 ammo, and food. |
| **Gunsmith Facility** | A fortified bunker/tunnel system. | **Gunsmith NPC** (Returns Day 10), workbench. |
| **The Warehouse** | An old farm building filled with horse remains. | Landmark for navigation, occasional floor loot. |
| **The Bridge** | A stone crossing over a small stream. | Dangerous choke point; often patrolled by bandits. |
| **Military Outpost** | Scattered ruined tanks and artillery pieces. | **Military crates**, weapon parts, and medical supplies. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** The central fields are death traps. Bandit marksmen have exceptional vision and will engage you from hundreds of meters away. Always hug the treeline or move between ruined vehicles.

## Looting Strategy: Where to Find the Best Gear

Looting the **road to vostok outpost map** requires patience. Unlike the urban areas, loot here is often tucked away in military crates or hidden within abandoned vehicles.

### The Sniper Tower
Located near the boundary, the tower is one of the safest places to find early-game supplies. At the very top, you can consistently find military crates containing 9x19mm ammunition and canned goods like tuna. It also serves as an excellent vantage point to scout the central field for enemy patrols before you make your move.

### The Gunsmith's Bunker
The Gunsmith is perhaps the most important NPC on this map. However, he is not always present. According to in-game events, the Gunsmith typically returns to the Outpost around Day 10. Near his facility, you will find a secondary shelter (bunker) that allows you to save your progress and manage your inventory. 

### Rescuing the Cat
A unique interaction on the Outpost map involves a "Rescue" mission for a cat found within the bunker/warehouse complex. Rescuing the cat is a wholesome distraction from the hardcore survival, though it requires you to safely navigate back to your home shelter without dying, as losing the cat in the field is permanent for that run.

## Survival and Combat Tactics

The AI on the Outpost map is significantly more aggressive than in the starting village. Bandits here are often equipped with Mosins and RK62s, allowing them to pin you down from a distance.

### Dealing with Marksmen
If you hear a shot and don't see the shooter, drop low immediately. While the game currently lacks a prone mechanic (as of the 2026 builds), crouching behind a ruined tank or a hay bale is your only defense. The marksmen often stand perfectly still in the tree line, appearing as only a few pixels. 

### Environmental Events: Fighter Jets
One of the most immersive features of the Outpost is the "Fighter Jet" event. With a roughly 25% chance of occurring, Finnish military jets will perform high-visibility flybys. While these are non-lethal, the deafening roar of the engines can mask the footsteps of approaching bandits, making it a dangerous time to be out in the open.

### Survival Essentials
| Item Type | Priority | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hydration** | **Critical** | The Outpost has very few water sources. Canned pears/peaches are vital. |
| **Splints** | **High** | A broken bone in the central field is a death sentence due to movement penalties. |
| **Optics** | **High** | Iron sights are insufficient for the long-range engagements common here. |
| **Tourniquets** | **Medium** | Essential for stopping heavy bleeds after a sniper hit. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you sustain a broken bone and are low on hydration, your health will drain rapidly. If you cannot reach the shelter, drop your most valuable loot on the floor of a recognizable landmark (like the Warehouse) so you can retrieve it after respawning.

## Advanced Navigation: The B Bank Key

To access the high-tier loot within the bunker systems of the Outpost, you will need the **B Bank Bunker Key**. This key is a rare spawn that can occasionally be found in the Generalist's building in the starting village or within the lockers at the School. Once obtained, it unlocks rooms filled with pristine weapon variants like the RK95 and specialized medical equipment.

## Transitioning to the Minefield

Once you have finished looting the **road to vostok outpost map**, your next destination is likely the Minefield. The transit point is located in the southeast corner of the map, past the farm facility. Ensure your hydration is above 50% before transitioning, as the Minefield requires slow, methodical movement that will drain your resources over time.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the Gunsmith on the Outpost map?**

A: The Gunsmith is located in the bunker facility near the center-north of the map. Note that he is an event-based NPC and usually only appears after Day 10 in the game world.

**Q: How do I survive a broken bone if I'm far from the shelter?**

A: Without a splint, your movement speed is drastically reduced. Your best bet is to consume any painkillers to stop the immediate HP drain and head straight for the nearest bunker. If death is inevitable, drop your gear in a safe corner to prevent it from despawning with your body.

**Q: Is there a map item I can find for the Outpost?**

A: Yes, map items can occasionally be found in the Generalist's shop or in trash bags throughout the School. However, they do not provide a "live" GPS location; you must still navigate using landmarks like the Sniper Tower and the Warehouse.

**Q: What should I do during the Fighter Jet event?**

A: Use the noise of the jets to your advantage. It is the perfect time to sprint across open ground or reposition during a firefight, as the AI's ability to "hear" your movement is effectively neutralized by the ambient noise.
