It's designed to help players stabilize their runs before venturing into more hostile territories.

Early Access launch maps within Area 05 include locations like **School**, **Highway**, and **Outpost**. These maps are designed for early-game exploration, allowing players to learn movement, looting, and risk management without the immediate threat of permadeath.

<Section icon={Target} title="Crossing the Border Zone: High-Stakes Transition">
Once you've gathered sufficient resources and equipment in Area 05, your journey will inevitably lead you to the Border Zone. This region acts as the treacherous crossing point between Finland and Russia, presenting significant challenges that demand careful planning and execution. The Border Zone is guarded by hostile forces and features various environmental hazards.

<Callout type="warning" title="Border Zone Dangers">
Each Border Zone map features unique crossing points with distinct challenges. Be prepared for mines, physical obstacles, waterways requiring boats, and heavily armed guards potentially supported by air units. Rushing this zone without proper reconnaissance is a recipe for disaster.
</Callout>

### Preparing for a Border Crossing

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gear Up Appropriately">
    Ensure you have adequate armor, a reliable weapon, and sufficient medical supplies. Consider bringing suppressors for stealth if your playstyle allows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scout the Route">
    If possible, gather intelligence on the specific crossing point you intend to use. Some might be less heavily guarded or have fewer environmental traps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Plan Your Exit Strategy">
    Know your extraction points and have a contingency plan if your primary route is compromised. The Border Zone is not a place for improvisation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Inventory">
    Only carry what is absolutely essential. While death here doesn't result in permadeath, losing valuable gear can set back your progression significantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Border Zone is a test of your preparedness and tactical skills. Success here means gaining access to the most lucrative, yet dangerous, part of the game: Vostok.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Vostok: The Permadeath Frontier and Ultimate Reward">
Vostok is the ultimate destination in Road to Vostok, a high-risk, high-reward permadeath zone located in Russia. This region holds the most valuable loot and is controlled by heavily armed Military forces, often utilizing armored vehicles. Dying in Vostok means losing **everything** – all your gear, progress, and even your character. This makes every push into Vostok an all-or-nothing gamble.

<Callout type="info">
The named Build 1 Vostok map is **Terminal**. This location is designed for players who have already mastered the Area 05 economy and successfully navigated the Border Zone.
</Callout>

| Aspect        | Description                                                                 | Strategic Implication                                 |
| :------------ | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Permadeath** | Death results in the loss of all items and character progress.              | Extreme caution, meticulous planning, high stakes.    |

| **Hostile Faction** | Controlled by the Military, equipped with heavy weaponry and vehicles.      | Avoid direct confrontation; use stealth and superior tactics. |
| **Route Planning** | Essential to identify entry and exit points, and potential safe zones.      | Minimize exposure, maximize efficiency of loot runs.   |
| **Resource Management** | Every item counts; prioritize high-value loot and essential survival gear. | Leave non-essentials behind to make room for rewards.  |

Approaching Vostok requires a fundamental shift in mindset. It's not just about surviving; it's about making calculated risks for significant payoffs. Only attempt the Vostok crossing when your base setup, gear, and recovery plan are fully optimized.
