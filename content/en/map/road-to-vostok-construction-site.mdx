## Combat Fundamentals That Matter More Than Raw Aim

In Road to Vostok’s grounded combat style, micro-decisions matter: where you stand, when you peek, and whether you reload now or after moving.

### Positioning priorities

- **Fight from cover that allows retreat**, not just cover that blocks bullets.
- **Avoid repeating peeks** from the same shoulder angle.
- **Limit silhouette exposure** on elevated or open surfaces.
- **Use sound pauses** to confirm enemy movement before pushing.

### Engagement checklist

| Decision Point | Ask Yourself | If “No” |
|---|---|---|
| Do I have retreat cover? | Can I disengage in 2 seconds? | Reposition before shooting |
| Am I hydrated and stable? | Can I survive prolonged fight? | Reset and heal first |
| Is my ammo appropriate? | Can this ammo finish armored targets? | Avoid direct contest; flank/leave |
| Is this fight required? | Does it improve my run objective? | Skip engagement |

> **⚠️ Warning:** In permadeath-linked loops, unnecessary fights are economic mistakes, not just mechanical mistakes.
