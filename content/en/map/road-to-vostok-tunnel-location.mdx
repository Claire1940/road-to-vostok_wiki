### Step-by-Step Entry Process

1.  **Locate the Tanker:** Find the large fuel tanker truck parked near the highway railing overlooking the tunnel entrance.
2.  **The Drop:** Carefully hop over the railing. You want to aim your fall so that you land near the undercarriage of the tanker or on the concrete ledge below.

4.  **Infiltration:** Once your leg is fixed, you can move directly into the bunker area that is normally sealed off.

> **Warning:** Never attempt this drop without at least one Splint and a Medkit. Being immobilized in the tunnel makes you an easy target for any wandering AI or players.

## Extracting Safely: The Ledge Jump

The biggest challenge of entering the tunnel via the "tanker drop" method is that the doors remain locked from the inside. Many players find themselves trapped with a backpack full of loot and no way out. However, there is a specific environmental exploit that allows you to climb back out to the highway level.

To the side of the main bunker area, there is a small concrete ledge. By sprinting and jumping toward this ledge, you can "mantle" or glitch your way back up to the railing level. This may take several attempts, so it is vital to keep an eye on your stamina bar.

### Extraction Tips
*   **Stamina Management:** Do not attempt the jump if your stamina is depleted from looting.
*   **Weight Check:** If you are carrying too much gear, your jump height may be slightly reduced. Consider dropping less valuable items if you cannot make the jump.
*   **The "Flick":** Some players find that flicking the mouse upward during the jump helps the character model catch the ledge more consistently.
