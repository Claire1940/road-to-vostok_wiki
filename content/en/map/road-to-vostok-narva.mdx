<Section icon={Target} title="Looting & Resource Management for Sustained Survival">
Effective looting and resource management are the backbone of any successful run in Road to Vostok, particularly when venturing into high-stakes areas. Knowing where to find the best gear, how to maximize your inventory, and how to keep your character healthy are non-negotiable skills.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Loot Locations">
    The **Village** is an excellent early-game zone, often yielding most items found in the game. For endgame loot, the **Vasto apartments** offer the highest rarity drops and numerous boxes containing powerful weapons and gear. Don't underestimate trash bags; they can contain surprisingly good items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scavenge Everywhere">
    Always check the floor, car seats, and every container. Loot often spawns in groups on the floor. Pay attention to specific container types; electrical boxes, for example, yield flashlights and batteries. Open everything to learn item spawn patterns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ammo & Weapon Management">
    Early on, always loot enemy weapons, remove their magazines, and keep the ammo. This ensures you have a constant supply. Ammo is light, allowing you to carry plenty. Selling enemy weapons to traders is a consistent way to earn currency for other supplies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trade & Tasks">
    Utilize traders for essential items. For instance, trade enemy weapons with the doctor for bandages, or fish with the generalist for early-game supplies. While tasks are useful for unlocking safe houses (like the school key and bunker key), don't rush them. Complete them organically as you find items.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Early Game Money Making">
Fishing is a safe and reliable method to make money early in the game. You don't need bait, just find fish pockets. The area behind the generalist is a known good spot. Trade your catches for useful items to kickstart your run.
</Callout>
