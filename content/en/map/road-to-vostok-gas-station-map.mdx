## Tactical Considerations and Scavenger Spawns

Approaching the gas station without a plan is a quick way to end your run. The **road to vostok gas station map** features several "kill zones" where AI scavengers have a significant advantage. ### Defensive Positions

If you find yourself pinned down inside the station, the back office provides the best defensive posture. It features a single entry point and a small window that allows for a view of the rear woods, which is a common flanking route for AI. Conversely, if you are attacking the station, the ridge to the southwest provides an excellent overwatch position, allowing you to clear the exterior before moving in.

> **💡 Tip:** Use a suppressed weapon when clearing the garage. The sound of unsuppressed gunfire at the gas station can attract scavengers from the nearby forest trails, leading to an overwhelming "pincer" maneuver.
