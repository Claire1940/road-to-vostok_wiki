### The Saline Hunt: Crafting the Water Lock

The core of the attic quest revolves around crafting a special item, often referred to as a "water lock" or "moonshine," which is then traded for the attic key. To craft this item, you'll primarily need to collect saline bottles for the Medic trader.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Scarcity Alert">
Saline bottles can be somewhat rare. Focus your looting efforts on medical cabinets, hospitals, and pharmacies. Engaging bandits can also yield unexpected medical supplies, so always be prepared for combat.
</Callout>

**Saline Gathering Locations:**

| Location Type       | Likelihood | Notes                                        |
| :------------------ | :--------- | :------------------------------------------- |
| **Medical Cabinets**| High       | Primary source, often found in houses        |
| **Hospitals**       | Very High  | Best chance, but usually high-risk areas     |
| **Pharmacies**      | High       | Dedicated medical loot zones                 |
| **Bandits/Enemies** | Medium     | Chance to drop from defeated foes            |
| **Air Drops**       | Low        | Random event, but can contain valuable meds  |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Crafting the Key Item">
Once you've gathered the necessary saline, you'll return to the Medic trader to exchange them for a key crafting component. This component, combined with other common items, will allow you to craft the final item needed for the General Goods trader.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deliver Saline to Medic">
    Locate the Medic trader. Turn in the required **three saline bottles** to complete their quest. In return, you will receive the "water lock" component.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Crafting Materials">
    Collect the remaining materials: a **bucket**, three bottles of **water**, some **sugar**, and **yeast**. These are generally easier to find through regular scavenging.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title='Craft the "Moonshine"'>
    Go to your crafting menu. Combine the "water lock" component, bucket, water, sugar, and yeast to craft the final tradable item (often resembling moonshine).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trade for the Attic Key">
    Head to the General Goods trader. Select their quest for the "bulkhead" or "attic key" and deliver the crafted item. The attic key will then be yours!
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Crafting Item | Quantity | Source / Method                                   |
| :------------ | :------- | :------------------------------------------------ |
| **Water Lock**| 1        | Quest reward from Medic (3x Saline)               |
| **Bucket**    | 1        | Found in various buildings, sometimes from traders |
| **Water**     | 3 bottles| Looted from buildings, water sources              |
| **Sugar**     | 1        | Looted from kitchens, shops                       |
| **Yeast**     | 1        | Looted from kitchens, shops                       |

### Strategic Placement of Storage

The attic is a blank canvas. Think about how you want to organize your loot. Do you want all weapons in one area, or categorized by type? Crafting materials near your workbench? Planning this out before placing containers will save you time later.

| Storage Type      | Ideal Contents                   | Placement Suggestions             |
| :---------------- | :------------------------------- | :-------------------------------- |
| **Large Lockers** | Weapons, armor, large components | Against walls, in corners         |
| **Fridges**       | Food, water, perishables         | Near entrance or crafting area    |
| **Cabinets**      | Ammo, small parts, meds          | Stacked, or along walls           |
| **Shelves**       | Tools, quest items, collectibles | Above tables, on empty walls      |
| **Tables**        | Active crafting items, temporary loot | Central, accessible areas         |
