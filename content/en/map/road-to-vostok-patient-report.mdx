<Section icon={Swords} title="Common Injuries and Status Effects">
The Finnish-Russian border is fraught with dangers, and your character will inevitably sustain various injuries. Recognizing these conditions and their symptoms is the first step towards effective treatment.

### Injury Types and Their Impact

| Injury Type    | Common Causes                               | Impact on Player                               | Recommended Treatment       |
|:---------------|:--------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------|:----------------------------|

<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Healing and Recovery Steps">
Effective healing isn't just about having the right items; it's about applying them strategically. Planning your recovery, especially after intense encounters, is vital for long-term survival.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess the Damage">
    After taking damage, immediately check your patient report. Prioritize life-threatening conditions like heavy bleeding or multiple fractures. A quick glance will tell you what needs immediate attention.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Find Cover and Secure the Area">
    Never heal in the open. Retreat to a safe, concealed location. Listen for enemy footsteps or patrol routes. Healing takes time, and being interrupted can lead to further injury or death.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Primary Treatment">
    Address the most critical issues first. Stop heavy bleeding with hemostatic agents or surgical kits. Immobilize fractures with splints. These actions prevent further health degradation and restore basic functionality.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Address Secondary Conditions">
    Once primary threats are contained, treat less immediate but still impactful conditions like dehydration, starvation, or minor cuts. Use bandages, food, and water to stabilize your overall health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Rest and Replenish">
    If possible, allow your character to rest in a shelter to naturally regenerate health and stamina. Replenish your medical supplies by crafting or trading before embarking on your next high-risk excursion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficient Medical Loadout">
Consider carrying a compact, versatile medical kit for quick battlefield use, and a larger, more specialized kit for shelter-based recovery. This dual approach maximizes both immediate response and long-term sustainability.
</Callout>

</Section>
