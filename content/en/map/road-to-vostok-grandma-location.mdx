## Navigating to the Road to Vostok Grandma Location

The search for the cat centers on the **Outpost** map. To reach this area, you generally follow the train tracks or move through the dense forest sections to avoid being spotted in the open. The AI in Road to Vostok is notoriously sharp, often spotting players from significant distances if they sprint across open fields.

Once you arrive at the Outpost, your target is the underground bunker system. While there are multiple bunkers scattered across the border zones, the cat is consistently located within the main Outpost bunker.

### Step-by-Step Bunker Access

1.  **Approach from the Woods:** Avoid the main roads where patrol vans are likely to spawn. Use the treeline for concealment.
2.  **Listen for Audio Cues:** Bandits will often talk or shout before they see you. Use this to pinpoint their locations before they form a flanking maneuver.
3.  **Locate the Bunker Entrance:** Look for the concrete reinforced stairs leading underground near the center of the Outpost complex.
4.  **Clear the Interior:** Bunkers are tight, CQC (Close Quarters Combat) environments. Use a flashlight or night vision if available, as these areas are significantly darker than the surface.

## Combat Strategies at the Outpost

The area surrounding the **road to vostok grandma location** is rarely empty. You will likely encounter "The Punisher" or groups of bandits that utilize aggressive flanking tactics. If you hear voices, stop moving and wait for them to reveal their position. In the current 2026 build, the AI tends to go silent once they have a confirmed visual on the player, attempting to ambush you as you exit buildings.

| Enemy Type | Threat Level | Behavior |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bandit Scavenger** | Low | Usually poorly armored; uses pistols or shotguns. |
| **Bandit Guard** | Medium | Equipped with rifles; will use cover effectively. |
| **The Punisher** | High | Heavy armor and high-tier loot; aggressive pursuit. |
| **Patrol Van** | Extreme | Mobile threat that drops off reinforcements. |

When fighting inside the bunker, use the corners to your advantage. Since the cat carrier takes up your "hands" or a large slot in your backpack, you need to ensure the area is 100% clear before you commit to picking it up. If you are spotted while carrying the cat, drop it in a safe corner, neutralize the threat, and then retrieve it.

## Inventory Management and Extraction

One of the most challenging aspects of finding the **road to vostok grandma location** is the journey home. The cat is not just a quest flag; it is a physical item that must be transported back to your hideout. 

If you find high-tier loot like an M4A1 or rare medical supplies in the bunker, you may face a difficult choice. Since the cat is essential for completing the Grandma's questline and unlocking further hideout improvements, it is generally recommended to prioritize the rescue over temporary gear upgrades.

> **Warning:** Do not attempt to use "Fly Cam" or other modded tools while carrying quest items like the cat. This can lead to save file corruption or the permanent loss of the item if the game crashes during a transition.

### The Return Trip
The safest route back is often the one you took to get there. Avoid the side of the train tracks where bandits are known to spawn in the 2026 update. Once you cross back into the starting zone, head directly to your hideout to secure the feline.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the road to vostok grandma location for the cat?**

A: The cat is located inside the main bunker at the Outpost map. You will need to head underground and clear out any residing bandits to find the cat carrier, which is usually found in the back rooms of the facility.

**Q: Does the cat take up a weapon slot?**

A: While it doesn't technically replace a weapon in your holster, it takes up a significant amount of grid space in your backpack. You may need to drop a secondary weapon or large loot items to fit the carrier.

**Q: Can the cat die during the extraction?**

A: Currently, the cat is treated as an invulnerable quest item while in your inventory. However, if you die, you will lose the cat along with the rest of your gear, requiring you to restart the mission from the Outpost.

**Q: What happens if the cat doesn't spawn in the bunker?**

A: In some versions of the early access, spawns can be randomized. If the cat is not in the primary Outpost bunker, check the secondary bunkers nearby. However, in the 2026 builds, the spawn is almost always guaranteed at the main Outpost location.

**Q: How do I feed the cat once it's in my hideout?**

A: You can interact with the cat in your hideout using the action key. If you have compatible food items in your inventory, an option to "Feed" will appear, which helps build the immersion of your survival base.
