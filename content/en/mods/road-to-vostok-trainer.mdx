## FAQ

**Q: Will using a road to vostok trainer get me banned?**

A: No. Road to Vostok is currently a single-player experience. There are no anti-cheat systems like BattlEye or Easy Anti-Cheat (EAC) that would result in a ban for using trainers or Cheat Engine in your own local session.

**Q: Why does my game crash when I activate "Infinite Ammo"?**

A: This usually happens if the trainer is outdated or if you are trying to freeze a value that the game engine is trying to reset (like during a reload animation). Try activating the cheat only after the weapon is fully loaded.

**Q: Can I use these cheats in the Vostok demo?**

A: Yes, most trainers and Cheat Engine methods are specifically tested on the public demo. Since the demo is the primary way to play right now, these tools are highly optimized for it.

**Q: Does the trainer fix broken bones or infections?**

A: Most basic trainers only affect the HP numerical value. To fix broken bones, you typically still need to use in-game splints, unless the trainer has a specific "Remove All Injuries" or "God Mode" toggle that bypasses status effects.
