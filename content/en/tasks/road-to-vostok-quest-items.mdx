## Rarity Tiers and Looting Strategy

Understanding item rarity is crucial for managing your stash. Most **road to vostok quest items** fall into three categories: Common, Rare, and Legendary.

1.  **Common (White):** Found in residential houses, sheds, and basic crates. Examples include Duct Tape, Lumber, and Books.
2.  **Rare (Blue/Green):** Found in specific containers like car trunks, medical bags, or toolboxes. Examples include Coffee, Saline, and Batteries.
3.  **Legendary (Gold/Purple):** These have a roughly 0.1% spawn rate in standard crates but are more common in Military or Special crates. Examples include the Mouth Organ, IFAKs, and the Water Lock.

> **⚠️ Warning:** In perma-death zones like Vostok and the Terminal, dying means losing your character and all currently carried items permanently. Always complete lower-tier tasks to gear up before attempting to collect high-risk quest items.

## How to Handle the Punisher Event

The Punisher is a unique boss encounter that has a 10% chance of triggering when you enter a zone. He travels in a lightly armored police van and is armed with a KP31 submachine gun. To complete the Gunsmith's final task, you must kill him and retrieve his **Flame Beanie**.

*   **Strategy:** You must damage the vehicle or stay in its line of sight until the occupants disembark. 
*   **Cover:** Never engage the Punisher in the open. His KP31 has a high rate of fire that can shred level 3 armor in seconds.
*   **Loot:** In addition to the quest item, the Punisher often carries the legendary KP31 and high-tier ammunition.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find Saline Solution for the Doctor?**

A: Saline is a rare medical item. While it can occasionally spawn in the School clinic, your best bet is checking the medical tents in the Outpost or looting the ambulances scattered along the Highway.

**Q: Do I lose my quest progress if I die in a perma-death zone?**

A: You do not lose the "Completed" status of a task, but you will lose any **road to vostok quest items** currently in your inventory that haven't been turned in yet. Always turn in items as soon as you collect them.

**Q: How do I get the Water Lock for the Generalist's "Homemade" task?**

A: The Water Lock is a legendary item with an extremely low spawn rate (0.1%). You can also craft it if you find three Saline Solutions, though finding the Saline can be just as difficult as finding the Lock itself.

**Q: Can I buy quest items from other traders?**

A: Some items, like the Scramma knives or basic Bandages, can be purchased once you have a high enough reputation. However, most legendary quest items must be found in the world or looted from specific AI enemies.
