## Common Mistakes Players Make With Road to Vostok Radio

Even skilled players misuse audio intel. Avoid these pitfalls:

1. **Over-translating uncertain words**  
   Partial translation can help, but forcing exact meaning from noisy clips leads to bad moves.

   Interference level, cadence, and transmission structure can be as important as vocabulary.

3. **Reacting to every anomaly equally**  
   Not all odd broadcasts deserve a full route change. Weight by repeatability.

4. **Failing to track outcomes**  
   If you don’t connect radio interpretation to survival results, your model won’t improve.

5. **Treating lore and tactics as separate**  
   In this game, narrative signals and practical risk often overlap.
