<Section icon={Swords} title="Enemy Management and Combat Tactics">

The Village is crawling with AI, and knowing how to engage them effectively is key to survival and completing your **Road to Vostok Village tasks**. Enemies can spawn dynamically, especially in central areas, making careful movement and clearing crucial. Never rush into an unknown building without first scouting the area.

<Callout type="warning" title="Middle Building Warning">
Avoid directly pushing the central building in the Village. Killing one AI there often triggers additional spawns, potentially overwhelming you with up to 10 enemies appearing randomly around your position. Clear the surrounding buildings first to prevent being flanked.
</Callout>

### Clearing Buildings Safely

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Approach">
    Approach buildings cautiously. Look for open doors, which often indicate an enemy nearby. Stay behind cover as you move.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Interior Sweep">
    Once inside, aim down sights and clear rooms systematically. Check common hiding spots like behind doors, in bathrooms, and behind furniture (e.g., sofas). Jump to peek over obstacles if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Vertical Clearance">
    In multi-story buildings, be especially wary. Enemies can spawn on upper floors, making them tricky to clear. Always clear the ground floor before ascending, and be prepared for enemies above.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Exiting Strategy">
    Before leaving a cleared building, quickly re-check entry points. New AI can sometimes spawn or patrol nearby. Close doors behind you to mark areas as cleared and prevent unexpected entries.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Task Management Strategy

To effectively manage your tasks, utilize the in-game note system. When you're in the task menu, you can add notes to individual tasks, reminding you of specific item requirements or objectives. This keeps you organized and focused during a raid.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Review Tasks Before Raid">
    Before deploying to the Village, check your active tasks. Note down essential items you need to find (e.g., "Doctor deliver duct tape and a tissue").
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Task Items">
    While looting, prioritize picking up items required for your current tasks. These items often have high value or unlock further progression.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use Task Notes">
    In the task menu, click on a task and select "Add Note." This allows you to jot down details like item quantities or specific locations if you know them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extract with Task Items">
    Ensure you successfully extract with all required task items. Failure to extract means losing progress on that specific task's item collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
