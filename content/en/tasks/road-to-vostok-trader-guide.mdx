His tasks are essential for setting up your home base with furniture and basic survival tools.

## The Doctor: Medical Mastery in the School

The Doctor operates out of the abandoned school's top floor. Reaching him is a challenge in itself, as bandits frequently patrol the hallways and surrounding forest. His tasks are vital because he starts with a 200% tax rate; every task completed reduces this by 20%, making essential medical supplies like antibiotics and bandages affordable.

### Navigating the School Safely
When approaching the Doctor, always clear the ground floor windows from a distance. Bandits often hide in the classrooms and will fire as you enter the corridor. Once inside, wait at least 10 seconds after a kill to ensure a second bandit isn't flanking you from a nearby room.

## The Gunsmith: Outpost Firepower

Located at the Outpost, the Gunsmith is the gatekeeper to high-tier weaponry. His bunker is situated between the tank and the sandbags on the left side of the map. Completing his tasks often requires venturing into perma-death zones like Vostok or the Terminal, but the rewards include some of the best ballistic protection and optics in 2026.

### High-Tier Combat Tasks
The Gunsmith's later tasks require finding "Legendary" items with a 0.1% spawn rate. Items like the SSV or the MK18 are exceptionally rare and often found only in military crates within high-risk areas.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The "Oil Samples" task for the Doctor requires visiting the Terminal. This is a perma-death zone. If you die here, your character and all gear are deleted permanently.

## Finding Rare Legendary Items

A major part of any **road to vostok trader guide** is explaining the spawn mechanics. Most task items like Saline, IFAX, and the SSV rifle are classified as Legendary. These have a roughly 0.1% chance to appear in any standard loot container. However, your odds increase significantly when searching:
1.  **Military Crates**: Found in the Border Zone and Vostok.
2.  **Special Crates**: Located deep within the Terminal.
3.  **The Punisher**: A boss that spawns in a police van (10% event chance).

If you are looking for the Oil Filter for the Generalist, prioritize searching the trunks of civilian cars. While rare, the engine bays and trunks are the primary spawn points for automotive components.

## Dealing with The Punisher

The Punisher is a unique world event. There is a 10% chance upon entering a zone that his armored police van will appear. To complete the Gunsmith's final task, you must intercept this vehicle.

*   **Tactics**: Do not stand in the open. The Punisher uses a KP31 with a drum mag and will shred unarmored players in seconds.
*   **The Kill**: Use high-caliber rounds to disable the vehicle or wait for the AI to disembark near points of interest like the School.
*   **The Loot**: Killing him grants his signature Flame Beanie and proves his death to the Gunsmith, unlocking the Storage Bunker.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I lower the trader tax in Road to Vostok?**

A: Each task you complete for a specific trader lowers their individual sales tax. For the Doctor, each task reduces the tax by 20%. Completing all tasks is the only way to reach the baseline market price for items.

**Q: Where can I find the Saline Solution for the Doctor?**

A: Saline is a Legendary item. It has a very low spawn rate in medical crates. Some players have better luck finding it in the "Special Crates" located in the Terminal perma-death zone or as a rare drop from medical bags in the School.

**Q: Is the Road to Vostok trader guide different for the demo?**

A: Yes, the 2026 Early Access version is significantly more "hardcore" than the original demo. Many items that were easy to find (like the Pike for the MK18) have been replaced with much harder requirements, such as finding three legendary IFAX kits.

**Q: What happens if I lose a task item before turning it in?**

A: If you die in a standard zone, you lose the items in your inventory but can try to find them again. If you die in a perma-death zone (Terminal/Vostok) while carrying a rare task item like the Oil Samples, that character is gone, and you must start the questline over with a new survivor.
