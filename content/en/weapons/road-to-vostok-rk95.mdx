## Comparative Analysis: RK95 vs. AK-74

While both rifles are common in the border regions, the choice between the RK95 and the AK-74 often comes down to caliber preference and availability of parts. The AK-74 uses the 5.45x39mm round, which offers lower recoil but less "punch" through cover than the RK95's 7.62mm rounds.

1.  **Damage Profile:** The RK95 wins in raw damage per hit, making it better for taking down armored AI.
2.  **Ammo Weight:** 5.45mm ammo is lighter, allowing you to carry more rounds for the AK-74.
3.  **Durability:** The **road to vostok rk95** generally has a higher durability ceiling in the 2026 build, meaning it jams less frequently when dirty.
4.  **Availability:** AK-74 parts are more common in the "Border" zone, whereas RK95 parts are often found deeper in Finnish-controlled areas.
