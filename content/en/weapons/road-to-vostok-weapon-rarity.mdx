Acquiring rare gear requires more than just luck. You must engage with the game's core systems: trading, tasks, and world exploration. The **road to vostok weapon rarity** can be bypassed if you build a strong relationship with specialized traders.

  <Step num="1" title="Build Trader Reputation">
