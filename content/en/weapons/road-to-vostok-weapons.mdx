### Essential Attachment Categories

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Optics: Enhancing Vision">
    Optics are divided into short, medium, and long-range categories. They allow for more precise aiming, target identification, and engagement at various distances. Examples include MRM, SRO, PRO, EXPS, Kobra, ACOG, and PU scopes. Choosing the right optic drastically improves your accuracy and situational awareness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Muzzle Devices: Suppressors & Brakes">
    Muzzle attachments primarily include suppressors (like Hybrid and Navy) and muzzle brakes. Suppressors reduce sound signature and muzzle flash, making you harder to detect, which is invaluable for stealthy approaches. Muzzle brakes, on the other hand, reduce recoil, improving control during sustained fire.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Magazines: Capacity & Reload Speed">
    While not always considered an "attachment," different magazine types can significantly impact your weapon's performance. Larger capacity magazines reduce the need for frequent reloads, crucial in intense firefights. However, they can also add weight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Underbarrel & Side Mounts: Utility & Ergonomics">
    These attachments include foregrips, laser sights, and flashlights. Foregrips improve weapon handling and reduce recoil, while laser sights offer quick target acquisition in close quarters. Flashlights are essential for navigating dark areas but can also reveal your position.
  </Step>
</Steps>
