<Section icon={Shield} title="General Steps for Installing Game Mods (Unofficial)">

Since official tools are not available, any modding attempts for Road to Vostok would fall under unofficial methods, often involving direct file manipulation. This process can be risky and may not always work due to game updates.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Backup Your Game Files">
    Before attempting any mod installation, **always create a backup** of your entire Road to Vostok installation folder. This allows you to revert to a clean state if something goes wrong. Navigate to your Steam library, right-click Road to Vostok, go to Properties > Local Files > Browse, and copy the entire folder to a safe location.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify Mod Sources">
    Look for community forums, Discord servers, or dedicated modding websites (if any emerge) where players might share unofficial modifications. Be extremely cautious about the source and ensure it's reputable to avoid malware.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Understand Mod Installation Instructions">
    Each unofficial mod will likely have specific installation instructions provided by its creator. These typically involve placing files into specific directories within your game folder. Read these instructions carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Install the Mod Files">
    Follow the mod's instructions to place the files in the correct locations. This might involve replacing existing game files, adding new assets, or editing configuration files.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test the Game">
    Launch Road to Vostok after installing the mod. If the game launches and appears stable, test the mod's functionality. If you encounter crashes or unexpected behavior, revert to your backup files.
  </Step>
</Steps>
