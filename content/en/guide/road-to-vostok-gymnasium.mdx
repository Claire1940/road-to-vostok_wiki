The **road to vostok gymnasium** is not just a loot spot; it is a test of your survival loop. Because the game features a multi-shelter support system, your goal should be to loot the gym and return to the nearest Area 05 shelter to save your progress and store your findings.

Shelters in *Road to Vostok* act as your only save points. If you die after a successful gym run but before reaching a shelter, you lose everything from that session. The closest shelter to the gymnasium is the "Suburban Cabin," located roughly 300 meters to the west.

<Callout type="info" title="Decoration Mode">
Items found in the gymnasium, such as chairs or small containers, can sometimes be picked up and used in "Decoration Mode" within your shelter. Look for items with the 'Interact' prompt to customize your home base.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Tasks and Progression">

Traders like the **Generalist** and the **Doctor** frequently issue tasks that require visiting the gymnasium. These objectives are the primary way to build your economy and unlock better gear for the Vostok push.

| Task Name | Objective | Reward | Trader |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Medical Retrieval** | Find 3 Saline Bags in the Gym Infirmary | $500 + Medkit | Doctor |

| **The Lost Signal** | Plant a beacon on the Gymnasium Roof | $1,200 | Scientist |

<Callout type="tip" title="Progression Bottleneck">
Many players get stuck on the 'Maintenance Access' task. The key is not always in the office; it can also spawn on the belt of the "Lead Bandit" who patrols the locker rooms.
</Callout>

Completing these tasks is vital for moving toward the **Border Zone**. The rewards often include the specialized gear—such as scuba equipment or mine detectors—needed to survive the crossing into the permadeath Vostok region.
