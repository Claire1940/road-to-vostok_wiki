Currently, the primary method for **road to vostok 回復** is the consumption of canned pea soup. Inside the small shed adjacent to this house, you will find cans of pea soup. A unique feature of the current demo is that you can exit and re-enter the map to respawn these items, allowing you to "grind" a stockpile of food before heading into more dangerous territory.

## Stamina Management and Physical Recovery

Survival in Road to Vostok isn't just about health; it's about managing your character's physical exertion. The game utilizes a dual-stamina system: **Body Stamina** and **Arm Stamina**. Understanding how these recover is vital for maintaining combat effectiveness.

### Body Stamina vs. Arm Stamina
*   **Body Stamina:** Drains while sprinting or jumping. If this hits zero, your character becomes exhausted, causing your arm stamina to plummet.
*   **Arm Stamina:** Drains while aiming down sights (ADS) or holding a weapon in a hip-fire ready position. High arm stamina is required for steady aim.

| Action | Stamina Drained | Recovery Method |
|---|---|---|
| **Jogging** | Low (Body) | Walk or stand still |
| **Full Sprint** | High (Body & Arm) | Stop moving and catch breath |
| **ADS / Hip Fire** | High (Arm) | Lower weapon or swap weapons |
| **Full Auto Fire** | Very High (Arm) | Use semi-auto or short bursts |

If your arm stamina is depleted, your weapon will sway violently, making it impossible to land shots. To facilitate faster **road to vostok 回復** of your arm stamina, try switching to your secondary weapon or putting your gun away entirely while moving between cover.

## Advanced Survival: Extraction and Map Transitions

One of the most important "hidden" mechanics for **road to vostok 回復** involves map transitions. In the current demo structure, successfully reaching a transition point (the edge of the map that leads to the next zone) acts as a full reset for your character's physical state.

When you cross into a new area or extract, your health is completely refilled to 100%, and your stamina reserves are restored. This makes "extraction streaks" a viable way to stay alive without consuming all your pea soup. However, this comes with a massive risk:
*   **Area 5:** If you die here, you lose your current inventory.
*   **Vostok Side:** If you die here, your entire save file is deleted.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never attempt to cross a transition point while under heavy fire. The animation and loading process can leave you vulnerable for a split second.

## Combat Tactics to Minimize Damage

The best way to handle **road to vostok 回復** is to avoid taking damage in the first place. The AI in Road to Vostok is programmed with specific limitations that you can exploit:

*   **Height Advantage:** AI struggles to aim at targets significantly higher or lower than them. Using the roof of the village buildings can provide a safe sniping vantage.
*   **The Crouch Glitch:** Currently, if you crouch while an AI is shooting at you from the same elevation, their accuracy drops significantly as they struggle to adjust their aim downward.
*   **Inertia Management:** Use the "Crouch Slide" by sprinting and then hitting the crouch key to quickly dive into cover. This reduces your profile and makes you a harder target to hit.
