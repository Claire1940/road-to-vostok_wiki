## FAQ

**Q: Can I lose my hideout once it is unlocked?**

A: No, once you have used the key to **road to vostok unlock next hideout**, the shelter remains available for the duration of that save file. However, if you are playing on Iron Man mode and die in the Vostok zone, your entire save file—including all unlocked hideouts—will be deleted.

**Q: Where do I find the alarm clock to skip time?**

A: The alarm clock is an item that can be looted or purchased from traders. Once you have it in your inventory or placed in your shelter, you can use it to set a specific time slot for the simulation to advance.

**Q: Do all hideouts have the same storage capacity?**

A: No. While all shelters allow you to place furniture, the physical dimensions of the shelters vary. Larger shelters in the Border Zone provide more floor space for storage crates and weapon racks compared to the initial starting cabins.

**Q: How many AI factions are there near the hideouts?**

A: There are three main factions: Bandits, Guards, and Military. Each has different equipment and AI behaviors. Generally, hideouts in Area 0-5 are surrounded by Bandits, while shelters closer to Vostok are patrolled by heavily armed Military units.
