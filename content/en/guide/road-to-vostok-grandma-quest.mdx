## Starting the Quest: The Trader's Shed

Before you can set out to find Grandma's house, you must first secure the task from the local trader. In the current 2026 build, NPCs and quest givers are primarily found in the starting shelters or the neutral "Shed" zones. These areas act as the hub for your operations, allowing you to trade scavenged loot for essential mission items.

To initiate the quest, follow these steps:
1.  **Locate the Trader:** Head to the central village area and find the small wooden shed near the pipe house.
2.  **Check the Task Board:** Interact with the NPC to view available deliveries and scouting missions.
3.  **Accept the Delivery:** Look for a task titled "Supplies for the Elder" or similar, which players commonly refer to as the Grandma quest.
4.  **Prepare Your Inventory:** Ensure you have enough space for the quest items, which often include medicine, food, or specific tools.

| Step | Objective | Key Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Accept Mission** | Visit the Trader in the Shed zone |
| **2** | **Gather Supplies** | Obtain **Medicine** or **Canned Pears** |
| **3** | **Navigate Village** | Avoid Bandit patrols in Area '05 |
| **4** | **Deliver Items** | Find the yellow cabin on the hill |

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the trader's inventory before leaving. If he is selling a **Mosin** or an **MP5**, prioritize buying them to defend yourself against the aggressive AI patrols you will encounter on the way.

## Locating Grandma's House

Finding the specific location for the **road to vostok grandma quest** can be difficult due to the game's lack of intrusive UI markers. The cabin is situated in the rural outskirts of the main village, typically tucked away behind a dense treeline to avoid detection by military patrols.

### Navigation Landmarks
*   **The Bridge:** Cross the main bridge leading away from the starter safehouse.
*   **The Yellow House:** Look for the two-story yellow building. Grandma's cabin is usually located 200 meters north of this landmark.
*   **The Forest Path:** Follow the dirt path that veers away from the asphalt highway. Avoid the open road to minimize visibility.

| Landmark | Tactical Significance | Danger Level |
|---|---|---|
| **Pipe House** | Good for early-game looting and medical supplies. | Medium |
| **Yellow House** | Contains high-tier loot but is often guarded by 2-3 Bandits. | **High** |
| **Grandma's Cabin** | The quest turn-in point; relatively safe once cleared. | Low |

## Combat and Survival Strategies

The **road to vostok grandma quest** isn't just a delivery run; it is a test of your combat readiness. The AI in Road to Vostok is notoriously "wonky" but lethal, often shouting in Finnish before opening fire. If you hear someone yelling "Satana!" or "Perkele!", take cover immediately.

### Recommended Loadout for the Quest
Success in this mission depends on your ability to engage at both medium and close ranges.
*   **Primary Weapon:** A **Mosin** with a PU Scope is ideal for picking off bandits guarding the village from a distance.
*   **Secondary Weapon:** An **MP5** or **AK-74** on single-shot mode is perfect for clearing the interior of the yellow house.
*   **Medical Supplies:** Carry at least two splints and a bandage. Leg injuries will slow you down, making you an easy target for snipers.
*   **Clothing:** Equip a **Winter Jacket** if the season has shifted to late autumn to prevent the "Coldness" debuff from draining your stamina.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt this quest at night without a flashlight. The darkness in Finland is absolute, and you are likely to wander into a minefield or a bandit camp without realizing it until it's too late.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is Grandma's house located in Road to Vostok?**

A: The house is typically a small, weathered cabin located north of the main village's yellow house. Look for a dirt path leading into the woods away from the main highway.

**Q: What happens if I die while doing the road to vostok grandma quest?**

A: Road to Vostok features hardcore mechanics. If you die in a normal zone, you lose the gear on your person but can return with a new character. If you are in the Vostok permadeath zone, your entire save file could be at risk depending on your difficulty settings.

**Q: Can I complete the Grandma quest without a gun?**

A: While technically possible to sneak, it is highly discouraged. The path to the cabin is usually patrolled by at least two bandits. It is better to scavenge enough loot to buy a basic pistol or find a shotgun before attempting the delivery.

**Q: Do quest items like medicine spawn in specific locations?**

A: Loot is randomized in each run, but medical supplies for the **road to vostok grandma quest** are most commonly found in the "Pipe House" or the bathroom cabinets of residential buildings in the village.
