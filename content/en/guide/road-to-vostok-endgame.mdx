One of the most overlooked features is the Arm Stamina system. Represented by a small arm icon under your main stamina bar, this drains whenever your weapon is in High Ready, ADS, or Canted fire modes. Keeping this bar full is the only way to ensure zero weapon sway during critical long-range engagements.

| Setting | Recommended Value | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Interpolate** | **OFF** | Removes mouse smoothing/delay for snappier aim |
| **Headbob** | **0%** | Increases visual clarity while moving |
| **Exposure** | **MAX** | Significantly improves visibility during night cycles |
| **Lean Keys** | **Mouse Buttons** | Allows for effective corner clearing while moving |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Aim Mechanic">
Never commit to a long-range shot if your Arm Stamina is below 50%. The resulting sway makes landing headshots nearly impossible, likely alerting the AI to your position without securing the kill.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Combat for Permadeath Survival">

In the **road to vostok endgame**, combat is not about who has the fastest trigger finger, but who has the most patience. The AI has superior vision and will often spot you through windows or across fields before you see them. To survive, you must adopt a "slow is smooth, smooth is fast" mentality.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visual Recon">
    Use lean peeking (shoulder peaking) for every corner. Never expose your full body until you have confirmed the room is clear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Force the Push">
    If engaged, retreat to cover and wait. The AI is aggressive and will eventually push your position, giving you an easy defensive kill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Aim Punch">
    If you take a hit, your character will flinch violently. Do not try to "shoot through" it. Get behind hard cover immediately to reset your aim.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Caliber Selection">
    By the time you reach Vostok, switch to high-caliber rifles. SMGs like the MP5 are excellent secondaries but lack the stopping power for endgame guards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Combat Element | Strategy | Effectiveness |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Canted Fire** | Use only with Laser Sights | ★★★☆☆ |
| **Full Auto** | Reserved for Indoors only | ★★★★☆ |
| **Lean Peeking** | Mandatory for every corner | ★★★★★ |
| **Single Fire** | Best for all Outdoor ranges | ★★★★★ |

<Callout type="success" title="Stealth Tip">
Pay close attention to audio cues. AI will often "talk to themselves" or emit subtle footstep sounds when trying to flank. In the endgame, your ears are more reliable than your eyes.
</Callout>
