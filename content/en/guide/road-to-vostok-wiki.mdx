<Section icon={Activity} title="Beginner Survival Guide: The First Session">

Surviving your first hour in the border zone requires a shift in mindset. You are not a super-soldier; you are a survivor. Your initial goal is to establish a stable economy in Area 05 before even looking toward the east.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Your First Shelter">
    Immediately locate a shelter in Area 05. Shelters act as your only save points and storage hubs. Use them to stash excess loot and stabilize your character's needs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scavenge for Basic Essentials">
    Focus on finding food, water, and medical supplies. Search containers in the School or Outpost maps. Physics-based loot means you should check every corner and shelf manually.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Weapon Handling">
    Practice reloading and checking chambers. The game uses realistic mechanics; if you don't manage your magazines and ammo types (like 9x18 vs 9x19), your gun will fail you in a firefight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Engage with Traders">
    Visit the Generalist or the Doctor. Trading is barter-based, so prioritize items that these NPCs value to upgrade your gear or buy specialized keys.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Tip">
Do not hoard everything on your person. If you die in Area 05, you lose everything in your inventory. Keep your most valuable items and spare weapons safely tucked away in your shelter's storage containers.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapons, Ammo, and Ballistics">

The combat system is built on tactical realism. Weapons are split into several families, each requiring specific ammunition and maintenance. Using the wrong caliber or neglecting attachments will significantly reduce your combat effectiveness.

| Weapon Family | Examples | Primary Calibers | Magazine Capacity |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Pistols** | C1911, G17, Makarov | .45 ACP, 9x19, 9x18 | 8 - 17 Rounds |
| **Rifles** | AK-12, AKM, MK18 | 5.45x39, 7.62x39, 5.56x45 | 30 Rounds |
| **Precision** | Mosin, SVD | 7.62x54R | 5 - 10 Rounds |
| **Shotguns** | RM-870 | 12/70 Gauge | 10 Rounds |

The ballistics system tracks individual rounds. For example, the **RM-870** shotgun is devastating within 2 meters but loses almost all effectiveness beyond 10 meters. Precision rifles like the **SVD** trade fire rate for high stopping power, making them ideal for clearing guards from a distance before attempting a border crossing.

<Callout type="info" title="Attachment System">
The game features PIP (Picture-in-Picture) scopes. You can equip various optics ranging from short-range red dots (SRO, PRO) to long-range scopes (ACOG, PU). Suppressors like the **Hybrid** or **Navy** are essential for stealthy players.
</Callout>
