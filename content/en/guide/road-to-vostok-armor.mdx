<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Acquisition and Bartering for Superior Armor">
Unlike many games, Road to Vostok operates on a pure barter system, meaning direct currency is non-existent. This fundamentally changes how you acquire better **Road to Vostok armor**. To get your hands on the best protective gear, you'll need to know what items traders value most and how to leverage your inventory effectively.

<Callout type="warning" title="Trader Mechanics">
Traders often charge double what they offer. Always show up with a diverse inventory of high-value items to secure favorable trades.
</Callout>

### Strategic Bartering Steps

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify High-Value Items">
    Focus on accumulating items like **ACOG sights, suppressors, electronics, elite medical supplies, and military gear**. These are highly sought after by traders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Looting">
    During your runs, target military loot spots and areas known for electronics. These locations are key to building up your trade inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Frequent Trader Visits">
    Trader inventories reset frequently. Make it a routine to farm valuable items, store them in your shelter, and revisit traders to check for updated stock of desired armor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Prepare for Trade-Ups">
    A single item is rarely enough for a good trade. Aim to have a loaded inventory of valuable items to exchange for high-tier weapons and **heavy armor** like the MK-18, SVD, or advanced plate carriers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By consistently following this loop, you can steadily upgrade your protective gear, making your character more resilient for the game's most dangerous zones.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Armor in Combat: Stealth vs. Brute Force">
