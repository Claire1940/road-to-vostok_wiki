## The Importance of Traders in Road to Vostok

In a world as unforgiving as Vostok, the economy is your best friend. Unlike many other extraction shooters, Road to Vostok places a heavy emphasis on the "transit" system, meaning you cannot simply click a menu to buy gear. You must physically travel to these NPCs, often crossing multiple map zones fraught with danger. 

Traders provide three essential services:
1.  **Gear Acquisition:** Buying "meta" weapons and high-tier armor.
2.  **Liquidity:** Converting found items like electronics or tools into currency.
3.  **Survival:** Purchasing medical kits and food to sustain your character during long raids.

## Finding the Generalist and the Doctor

Before you can reach the high-end gear, you must master the starting zones. Most players will begin their journey at the beginner cabin. From here, your first goal is the School Zone. This area acts as a hub for early-game progression and contains the first two major **road to vostok trader locations**.

### Navigating to the School Zone
To reach the traders in the School Zone, leave your cabin and head toward the waterline. Follow the coast until you encounter a perimeter marked with yellow tape. At this point, cut left into the interior of the map. Alternatively, if you are moving quickly, you can head straight through the woods diagonally from the cabin to reach the heart of the school district.

### Trader Services in the Hub
The Generalist is usually your first stop. He buys almost everything you find in the initial cabins and houses. The Doctor is located further down the main road, past the primary school building. It is highly recommended to stock up on at least two heavy bandages before attempting to move to the next zone, as bandits in the Outpost use higher caliber rounds.

> **💡 Survival Tip:** Always check the Doctor's inventory for specialized injectors. In 2026, these can provide temporary stamina boosts that are vital for crossing the open highways between zones.

## How to Find the Weapon Specialist (Outpost Location)

The Weapon Specialist is arguably the most important NPC for mid-to-late game players. He is the sole provider of "meta" weapons and, more importantly, body armor. Without armor, a single bandit with a pistol can end your run instantly.

To find the Weapon Specialist, you must transit from the School Zone to the Outpost. This is a linear path but highly exposed. Follow these steps to ensure you arrive safely:

1.  **The Highway Run:** Stay on the main highway leading away from the school. 
2.  **The Transit Point:** Look for the far end of the map beyond the Doctor's location. You will find a transit icon that leads to the Outpost.
3.  **The Outpost Shelter:** Once you spawn in the Outpost, move left until you find a tent shelter. This is the second permanent shelter in the game and serves as a vital save point.
4.  **The Rock Formation:** Move past the shelter toward the edge of the map, then take a right.
5.  **The Bunker Entrance:** Look for a tank parked in front of a bunker built directly into the side of a rock formation. The Weapon Specialist is located inside this bunker.

## Advanced Trading Tactics and Resets

Managing the **road to vostok trader locations** efficiently involves understanding the game's internal clock. In the 2026 version of Road to Vostok, trader inventories do not refresh instantly. If you are looking for a specific high-tier item, such as a suppressor or a level 4 armor plate, you might find the "Out of Stock" message.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not linger in the Outpost for too long while waiting for a reset. Bandit patrols in this zone are significantly more aggressive and better equipped than those in the School Zone.

### Inventory Rotation
If the Weapon Specialist is sold out, it is often safer to retreat back to the School Zone shelter, sleep for 12-24 in-game hours, and then return. This forces a shop refresh. Always ensure you have enough currency (Vostok Credits) before making the trek, as the transit between maps consumes precious calories and hydration.

### Selling for Maximum Profit
Not all traders buy items at the same price. While the Generalist will buy almost anything, specialized items like broken weapon parts or medical chemicals may fetch a better price if held until you reach the appropriate specialist.

1.  **Medical Supplies:** Sell to the Doctor for a 15% bonus.
2.  **Weapon Parts/Ammo:** Sell to the Weapon Specialist for the best rates.
3.  **General Loot:** Electronics, tools, and household items go to the Generalist.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the best place to find armor in Road to Vostok?**

A: The Weapon Specialist is the primary source for body armor and plates. He is located in the Outpost map, inside a bunker near a parked tank. Exploring other **road to vostok trader locations** like the Generalist will only provide very basic or low-durability gear.

**Q: Do traders move their locations?**

A: No, as of the 2026 update, trader locations are fixed within their respective maps (School Zone and Outpost). However, your ability to access them depends on clearing nearby bandit threats.

**Q: How do I reset a trader's inventory?**

A: Trader inventories typically reset every 24 in-game hours. You can pass the time by sleeping in a shelter, such as the one near the Weapon Specialist in the Outpost or your starting cabin.

**Q: Is there a trader in the starter area?**

A: No, there are no traders at the starting cabin. You must at least transit to the School Zone to find the Generalist and the Doctor. This makes the initial trek one of the most critical parts of a new run.
