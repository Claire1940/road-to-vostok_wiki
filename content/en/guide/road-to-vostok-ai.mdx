<Section icon={Target} title="Tactics for Engaging Different AI Types">

Engaging the various AI factions in Road to Vostok requires different tactical approaches. Rushing a military patrol like you would a bandit camp is a recipe for disaster. Here's how to adapt your combat strategies for each enemy type.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Dealing with Bandits">
    **Bandits** are often less armored and use a variety of scavenged weapons. They might try to flank you or overwhelm you with numbers.
    - **Prioritize**: Headshots and quick elimination to prevent being surrounded.
    - **Gear**: SMGs, pistols, and shotguns are effective in close-quarters encounters common in Area 05.
    - **Avoid**: Prolonged engagements if outnumbered; reposition and pick them off from advantageous positions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigating Guards">
    **Guards** are more disciplined and equipped. They hold static positions and patrol routes, often working in conjunction with environmental hazards.
    - **Prioritize**: Reconnaissance. Identify patrol patterns, minefields, and sniper nests before engaging.
    - **Gear**: Medium-range rifles with optics and suppressors for stealth takedowns are highly effective. Explosives can clear obstacles or dislodge entrenched enemies.
    - **Avoid**: Direct frontal assaults. Use cover, concealment, and flanking maneuvers. Be wary of their callouts and potential air support.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confronting the Military">
    The **Military** in Vostok represents the pinnacle of AI threat. They are heavily armed, armored, and operate with tactical precision, often supported by vehicles.
    - **Prioritize**: Evasion and long-range engagements. Direct combat is extremely risky.
    - **Gear**: High-caliber sniper rifles, heavy armor, and advanced optics are essential. Anti-vehicle weapons (if available) are crucial for armored threats.
    - **Avoid**: Any engagement unless absolutely necessary and with a clear escape route. Focus on high-value targets if you must fight, and never linger.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Preparation is Key">
Always check your gear, ammunition, and medical supplies before venturing into a new area. The AI won't wait for you to be ready.
</Callout>
