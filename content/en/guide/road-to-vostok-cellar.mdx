<Section icon={Warehouse} title="Mastering the Road to Vostok Cellar: Storage and Safety">

Storage in your **road to vostok cellar** is not just a menu; it is a physical interaction. You can place items individually on shelves, stack crates in corners, or use specialized furniture containers. Because items are physics-based, you must be careful not to clutter your walking paths, as this can lead to "physics explosions" where items fly across the room upon loading.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Securing Your First Shelter">

When you first spawn in Area 05, you are vulnerable. Finding and securing your first **road to vostok cellar** should be your absolute priority. Follow these steps to establish your base of operations in the 2026 build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Starter Map">
    Begin in Area 05 maps like **School** or **Highway**. These regions are designed for early-game progression and contain the highest density of basic shelters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Immediate Perimeter">
    Before entering a potential shelter, check for nearby Bandits. You don't want to be followed into your safe zone, as enemies can linger outside the door.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initialize the Save Point">
    Enter the shelter and locate the interaction point (usually a bed or a laptop). This will register the shelter as your active spawn point and save your current inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Set Up Basic Logistics">
    Move a storage crate near the door. Empty your backpack of all non-essential items like crafting components or trade goods to maximize space for your next run.
  </Step>
</Steps>
