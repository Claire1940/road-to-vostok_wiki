## Map Layout and Initial Infiltration

Upon spawning into the Terminal area, players are immediately met with a sprawling industrial complex characterized by warehouses, shipping containers, and multi-story structures. The layout is designed to funnel players into "kill zones" where AI defenders have the height advantage. 

The spawn point typically places you on the periphery of the main industrial yard. From here, your first priority is concealment. The terrain is littered with patches of grass and debris; using these as cover while you scan the rooftops is the difference between a successful raid and a quick trip back to the menu.

### Strategic Entry Points

| Entry Route | Risk Level | Loot Density | Primary Threats |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Main Gate** | High | Low | Roof Snipers, Open Ground |

> **Warning:** Avoid the open gates near the center unless you have already cleared the surrounding rooftops. The lack of ground-level cover makes you an easy target for AI stationed in the upper windows.

## AI Spawn Logic and Combat Tactics

The AI in the **road to vostok terminal map** behaves differently than in the open-field maps like Village. Here, the AI utilizes the industrial architecture to pin players down. Understanding their spawn logic is vital for survival.

Usually, you will encounter at least three initial enemies upon entering the yard. A common configuration includes one AI defender on the roof of each major building. These snipers have a wide field of view and can spot you from significant distances. 

### AI Hotspots and Behavior

*   **Rooftop Guardians:** These enemies stay stationary but have high accuracy. Always look up before crossing an alleyway.
*   **Ground Patrols:** Often found walking near the military crates or inside the central area. They tend to stick to the shadows and can be heard by their footsteps on the gravel.
*   **Interior Defenders:** Occasionally, AI will spawn inside the warehouse rooms. These encounters are high-risk due to the tight spaces.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the "Slow Lean" mechanic to clear corners in the warehouse. AI reaction times in 2026 are sharp, and sprinting around corners is a guaranteed way to take damage.

## Navigation and Extraction Strategy

Traversing the middle of the map is the most dangerous part of the journey. To reach the extraction or the transition to the Apartments, you must pass through the central corridor.

1.  **Clear the Perimeter:** Use a suppressed weapon to take out the rooftop snipers from a distance.
2.  **The Middle Doorway:** Use the side doors rather than the main bay doors. The side doors offer more immediate cover once you step inside.
3.  **Collect Essentials:** Search the floor near the center of the hub for Oil Samples. These are often tucked near the industrial machinery or lumber piles.
4.  **Rooftop Ascent:** If you have cleared the area, head to the top floor of the central building. There is a special crate here that often contains the best loot on the map, though it leaves you exposed to any late-spawning AI.

## Advanced Survival Tips for 2026

*   **Check the Ground:** High-value items like Lumber and Oil Samples aren't always in crates. In the 2026 version of the map, many quest items are placed dynamically on the floor near industrial shelves.
*   **Sound Cues:** The Terminal has a lot of metal surfaces. Listen for the "clink" of boots on metal stairs to identify if an AI is moving to a rooftop position or coming down to flank you.
*   **Shadow Management:** The industrial lighting creates deep shadows. Use your flashlight sparingly, as it gives away your position to rooftop snipers, but keep it ready for clearing the dark corners of the warehouse.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find Oil Samples on the road to vostok terminal map?**

A: Oil Samples are primarily found in the middle section of the map. Check the ground near the doorways of the central industrial hub and around the lumber piles. They are often loose loot rather than inside crates.

**Q: How many AI enemies usually spawn in the Terminal?**

A: While spawns can vary, you should typically expect at least 3-5 AI. Usually, there is one on the roof of each major building and 1-2 patrolling the ground level near the military crates.

**Q: Is the Terminal map safe for night raids?**

A: Night raids are extremely difficult due to the verticality. AI snipers can be hard to spot in the dark, even with night vision, as they often hide behind rooftop vents and AC units. It is recommended to learn the map during daylight hours first.

**Q: Can I transition back to the Apartment map from here?**

A: Yes, there is a path that leads back towards the apartment zone. It is located past the central warehouse area, but you should ensure the rooftops are clear before making the sprint to the transition point.
