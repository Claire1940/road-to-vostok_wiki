## Combat Tactics: Survival in the School Zone

The AI in the School zone is more aggressive than the scavengers found in the early-game forests. They utilize cover effectively and will often attempt to flank you if they hear unsuppressed gunfire.

One of the unique mechanics in *Road to Vostok* is how weight affects your stamina and movement. While the classroom and gymnasium contain heavy loot, you must be careful not to overencumber yourself.
