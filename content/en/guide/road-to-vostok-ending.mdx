### Strategic Movement and Combat Tips

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Embrace the Darkness">
    At night, avoid using your flashlight. While it provides immediate visibility, it also acts as a beacon for enemies, making you visible from hundreds of meters away. Move slowly, rely on your hearing, and let your eyes adapt to the low light. Darkness is your best cover.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Respect Enemy AI">
    Never rush into close-quarters combat. *Road to Vostok*'s AI is programmed to punish aggressive, run-and-gun tactics. Always seek cover, maintain distance, and take calculated shots. Think like a sniper, not an action hero.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Your Arm Stamina">
    During prolonged aiming or rapid firing, your character's arm stamina will deplete, leading to drastically reduced accuracy. Watch the arm icon. If it turns red, briefly lower your weapon to recover stamina, then re-engage with renewed precision.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Be Mindful of Weight">
    The game's weight system is unforgiving. Being overweight significantly slows you down, drains stamina, and impairs aiming, turning you into an easy target. Prioritize essential loot and ensure you can always run, dodge, and reposition effectively.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Understand Respawns">
    Every time you transition between zones, all loot and enemies in those areas respawn. Never assume an area is safe after you've cleared it once you've left and returned. Treat every re-entry as if it were the first time, approaching with caution.
  </Step>
</Steps>
