When approaching the School, the safest route is generally from the left side of the map. This flank offers more natural cover and allows you to scout the perimeter before committing to an entry. The area is characterized by a large main building, a smaller outbuilding, and various military-grade containers scattered throughout the yard.

It is highly recommended to use a weapon like the MP5 or a versatile automatic rifle. However, discipline is key. You should inspect your weapon to verify your fire rate before engaging. In the tight corridors of the school, single-fire mode is often more effective for conserving ammunition and ensuring headshots against the 2-3 enemies that typically spawn in this zone.

## Entering the School: Keys and Points of Interest

Once you have reached the perimeter, your primary objective is usually the interior rooms. However, the School is not entirely open. Several high-value rooms are locked and require specific keys that can be found as random world spawns or on defeated enemies.

### The Classroom and Gymnasium
The two most important locked areas are the Gymnasium and the Classroom. The Classroom is particularly vital because it is the primary spawn location for the Medic trader. 

*   **Classroom Key:** Required to access the Medic. This key is a rare spawn, so check every jacket and filing cabinet in the surrounding houses.
*   **Gymnasium Key:** Opens the large sports hall which often contains military crates and high-tier gear.

### Finding the Medic Trader
The Medic is one of the few friendly NPCs you will encounter. Trading with him is the most reliable way to acquire professional-grade bandages, medkits, and stims. He is usually tucked away in a corner of the classroom, but be careful—the room isn't always "safe" until you've cleared the immediate hallway.

> **💡 Tip:** If you have a sniper rifle, use the elevated terrain on the left side of the school to pick off the guards inside the big house before you move into the school courtyard.

After you have finished your business at the School, you need a plan to leave. The "Outpost" transit point is located nearby. To reach it, head past the school buildings toward the map boundary. Ensure you are not over-encumbered, as the weight of the loot can make you a slow target for any roaming scavengers near the exit.

## FAQ

**Q: Where exactly is the School located in Road to Vostok?**

A: The School is located at the site of the former minefield transit. It is a large complex that serves as a central hub for the Medic trader and military-grade loot.

**Q: Can I find the Classroom Key inside the School?**

A: While keys can occasionally spawn inside the building they unlock, it is more common to find the Classroom and Gymnasium keys in the surrounding residential houses or as random loot on enemies in the area.

**Q: What is the best way regarding road to vostok how to get to school without being seen?**
