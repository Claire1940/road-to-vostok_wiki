Survival in **Road to Vostok PvE** extends beyond just combat; it encompasses meticulous resource management, strategic trading, and effective base building. Every decision, from what you carry to where you establish your safe house, impacts your longevity.

### Resource Management and Player Stats

Maintaining your character's well-being is critical. You'll need to monitor several stats to stay alive and effective. Neglecting these can lead to mental degradation or physical collapse.

| Stat Name          | Impact on Gameplay                                   | Mitigation Strategies                                      |

<Callout type="tip" title="Physics-Based Looting">
The game features a physics-based looting system where items don't spawn in fixed locations. Search every container and interactable object; nothing is guaranteed to be in the same spot twice, encouraging thorough exploration.
</Callout>

**Road to Vostok PvE** operates without a traditional currency. Instead, players engage in a barter system with traders, exchanging items based on their perceived value in a post-apocalyptic world.

| Trader Task Example        | Required Items                       | Reward Item Category                     |
|:---------------------------|:-------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------|
| "Medical Supply Run"       | Specific medical items (e.g., painkillers, bandages) | Weapon attachments, rare food            |
| "Scavenger Hunt"           | Common household items (e.g., toilet paper, soap) | Shelter customization items, basic tools |
| "Weapon Part Exchange"     | Damaged weapon parts, specific caliber ammo | Functional weapons, advanced gear        |
